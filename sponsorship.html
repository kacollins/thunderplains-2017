Sponsorship Packages & Information

Target Audience

ThunderPlains is a developer-focused conference. Our audience is mostly JavaScript and front-end developers and related jobs. JavaScript is unique in the fact that all developers have to use it in the browser, so it's not uncommon to have a wide variety of developers with all kinds of different experience and backgrounds at our conference.

  • Frontend JavaScript (Browsers)
  • Backend JavaScript (Node.js)
  • Frontend Developers and Designers (HTML/CSS, etc.)
  • Developers from all backgrounds learning more/better JavaScript
  • Business/Startup Owners
  • Project Managers

Attendance

For 2017, we are capping ticket sales at 325. This will be the largest attendee count we have ever had. We anticipate selling out.
