What Is ThunderPlains?

ThunderPlains is a web and mobile developer conference that focuses on JavaScript and related technologies that make the web interesting.

ThunderPlains is organized and backed by the Techlahoma Foundation – a volunteer-run 501(c)3 non-profit community foundation.

Call For Papers

Our call for papers is open! If you are interested in speaking at ThunderPlains this year, please submit a few talks!

There will be two tracks this year - a main room (300) for headline talks of wide appeal, and the tasting room (60) for deeper technical talks, or talks for a specific audience that may not have as broad appeal. So even if your talk may only appeal to a subset of our developer-focused audience, please submit anyways – our venue is perfect for your talk!

Speaker Package

Speakers receive 2 night hotel stay, travel (roundtrip airline tickets, gasoline, or preferred method), conference tickets for themselves and an optional +1, speaker+volunteer dinner, plus reimbursement for meals and entertainment.

Conference Organizers are available to book your travel needs, upon request.

Code of Conduct

Before attending the conference, please review the Thunder Plains Code of Conduct The CoC applies to all attendees, speakers, volunteers, and vendors at official and unofficial events by Techlahoma and any location where attendees may be congregating. Contact us if you have questions.
